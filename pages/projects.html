All of the Lights

All of the Lights is a device that instantly creates a light show with any music source and lights that includes a custom GWTC PCB and compact box.

LED Cube Shield

This is a custom-designed and self-contained 3x3 LED Cube shield for an Arduino with a Python GUI for easy pattern creation.

555 Timer Mini Synth

This simple electronic instrument on a custom-designed GWTC PCB packs eight notes into a compact package roughly the size of a 9V battery.

HackGW Project

This is a simple MIDI-keyboard controlled strip of RGB LEDs that responds with a different color based on the keys that are played, made at the HackGW Hackathon.
