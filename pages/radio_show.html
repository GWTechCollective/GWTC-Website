The Tech Collective

GW Tech Collective's own radio talk show! The Tech Collective is a group of students really excited about science, technology, and engineering. We talk about the latest trends in technology, we're nerdy, and we have a great time. If you took your laptops to prom like we did, this show is for you. Our hosts Elliot Liskin, Christoph Von Braun, and Dimitri Bleifer chat every week on WRGW about all things Tech.
